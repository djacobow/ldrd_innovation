Dominant Design Overview

Over the years, a dominant design has emerged, which consists of 3 blades, mostly up-wind rotor, pitch-control for braking and an integrated gear box.

Innovations in rotor design: Two of the major innovations in rotor design were determined by government testing: (1) increasing rotor radius/blade-swept area while maintaining reliability, and (2) blade (airfoil) design and composition.
